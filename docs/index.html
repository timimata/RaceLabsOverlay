Ghost Racing

Record your best laps and race against your ghost in real-time. Visual delta shows exactly where you're gaining or losing time.

60Hz Real-time

Ultra-smooth 60Hz refresh rate ensures you see every detail of your telemetry data without any lag or stuttering.

🎨

Customizable UI

Drag and drop widgets anywhere on screen. Edit mode lets you position everything perfectly for your setup.

Always stay up to date with automatic updates. New features and improvements delivered seamlessly.
